    ~The Community Hub - PRIVACY POLICY~



    Protecting Your Privacy

    The Community Hub (TCH) is committed to provide it’s members with the best of services and highest levels of member satisfaction. This includes but not limited to protecting their privacy. The Community Hub is bound by the Privacy Act 1988, which has set out sufficient principles concerning the protection of member’s personal information.
    Set out in the following is the information that The Community Hub is required to communicate to its members. The Community Hub recommends that you review this information and keep visiting our website as and when it is required by you in future for reference.

    Your personal information

    Personal information provided by you or your authorized representative/s to The Community Hub may include name, address, mobile/ telephone number/s, email address, debit/credit card or bank account details, ABN, driver’s license number and your credentials like The Community Hub PIN, username or password. The Community Hub also keeps all necessary details related to your The Community Hub services (including status as well).If you opt not to provide certain compulsory personal information, The Community Hub may not be in a position to provide you with the service/s you request, or in some cases the level of service/s on which The Community Hub claims pride itself.
    The Community Hub believes that you shall keep informed your representative/s that you are providing their personal information to The Community Hub and to advise them all that The Community Hub can also be contacted for any further information (see “How to contact us” given below).

    How we collect personal information

    The Community Hub may collect personal information in any of following ways:
    • from you directly, when you provide your information over a phone call or in some application form/s, or whenever you submit such details through The Community Hub’s website;
    • from sources of information available publicly;
    • in response to a survey conducted by The Community Hub;
    • from TCH’s own records of how you or your representative/s use our TCH service/s; or when it is legally required to do so.

    How we use your personal information

    The Community Hub may use your personal information to:
    • verify the identity;
    • assist while you getting subscribed to The Community Hub’s current service/s;
    • offer you and or provide you with additional or related The Community Hub service/s;
    • manage these services, including but not limited to charging, billing or collecting debts;
    • advise you of appropriate ways the services provided to you could be further improved;
    • conduct certain checks for credit-worthiness or for fraud;
    • conduct certain police character record check;
    • gain clear understanding of your information and communication requirements in order that The Community Hub can provide you with relatively better service; and
    • maintain and develop The Community Hub’s systems and infrastructure, including audit / test or upgrade of these systems.
    • Security
    The Community Hub has taken concrete steps to keep secure any confidential information it holds. Personal information is stored only in a secure server using secure file system. All credentials, personal and financial information are secured with advance level of encryption and authorization of its access to very limited people.

    Unauthorised Access and Disclosure

    The Community Hub shall not disclose any of your personal information to any unauthrised persons or entities. This includes but not limited to request for credentials like account passwords or login details. The Community Hub never give out any of Logins and passwords via phone, these can only be requested by the account holder via an online ticket using password reset tool.

    Review and changes to this Privacy Policy

    The Community Hub has standardized its policy review bi-annually. Subsequent to any change an amended version shall be posted on our website. We suggest that you visit this page as and when required for your updated reference.

    How to contact us

    Feel free to contact us for any questions, complain or feedback. You can reach the management directly at info@The Community Hub.xxx.xx
